ABOUT ME

My name is Boryana Angelova, and I am 27 years old. I've been skiing since the age of 5, thanks to my parents who are passionate skiers themselves. Three years ago, I embarked on my journey into the world of freeride skiing, discovering a profound love for the challenges of the mountains. Over time, I've gained significant experience through participation in various competitions and training sessions. For me, freeride skiing is more than just a sport, it's a way of life that inspires me to push my limits whenever I'm in the mountains. My passion is not only expressed in my desire to win but also in my constant thirst for improvement and new challenges. I share my sports experience and aim to continue challenging myself while contributing positively to the freeride culture in Bulgaria. In the last two years, I've had the pleasure of competing at a high level in various freeride competitions. I am proud to share that in Bansko, in front of my home audience, I achieved outstanding results. In 2022, I stood on the podium at the top of the Bansko Freeride World Qualifier 3*, and the following year, I again secured the first place at the Bansko Freeride World Qualifier 3*. These achievements are particularly meaningful to me as they represent not only personal accomplishments but also the opportunity to share this experience with the local audience. Their presence and support contribute to my inspiration and motivation, and I am grateful to represent Bulgaria on the world stage of freeride. This journey is not just for me but for everyone who supports and believes in my abilities.

Boryana Angelova


In the 2022–2023 season, I secured a commendable 4th place in highly competitive fields at both JAM EXTREME FWQ 3* and Nendaz Freeride Qualifier 3*. These experiences provided invaluable lessons and ignited my inspiration for continuous improvement. Furthermore, I delivered a strong performance at the French Freeride Series Avoriaz Qualifier 3* and La Rosière Qualifier 4*, solidifying my pride in my abilities. Despite enjoying my successes in Bulgaria, I am grateful for the opportunity to compete against global talents. These two fourth-place finishes outside Bulgaria are an inevitable part of the learning journey toward achieving the ultimate goal. I eagerly anticipate the challenges ahead and am ready to continue growing and pushing myself in every situation. I’m a dedicated freeride skier with a passion for pushing boundaries.
